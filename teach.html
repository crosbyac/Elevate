Toronto Noise Bylaw

The Noise Bylaw provides standards for noise and applies to all properties in Toronto.

Construction hours:

• Monday to Friday between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m.
• Saturday from 9 a.m. to 7 p.m.
• Not allowed on Sundays and statutory holidays.

The sound emitted from any equipment shall not exceed an equivalent sound level of 85 dBA when measured 20 metres from the source over a five-minute period.

About Noise Complaints

After you file a noise complaint, a bylaw officer will be back in touch within five days.

To assist in the investigation, you may be asked to provide a noise log and record the date and times you hear the noise. (We do that for you!)

Concerned about a current noise disruption and wondering if it’s worth filing a complaint? Test your environment to see if it exceeds Toronto bylaw compliance.
